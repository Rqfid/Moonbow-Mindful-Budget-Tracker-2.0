❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Apa itu Moonbow?
Moonbow adalah mindful budget tracker yang mengutamakan kesadaran (awareness) dibanding kontrol ketat. Kami percaya financial wellness datang dari koneksi dengan nilai pribadi, bukan disiplin yang keras.
Bagaimana data saya disimpan?
Semua data tersimpan di localStorage browser Anda secara otomatis. Tidak ada akun, tidak ada server, tidak ada cloud. Data 100% pribadi dan tetap di perangkat Anda. Pastikan tidak menghapus browser data jika ingin menyimpan progress.
Apa bedanya NEEDS, WANTS, dan JOYS?
NEEDS: Kebutuhan esensial (makanan, transportasi, tagihan).
WANTS: Keinginan yang bisa ditunda (gadget, pakaian baru).
JOYS: Hal yang membawa kebahagiaan sejati (hobi, pengalaman berharga).
Bagaimana cara kerja Moon Phases di Saving Tracker?
Setiap saving goal memiliki 8 moon phases. Setiap kali Anda menabung, klik moon berikutnya. Moon akan berubah warna (filled) dan menampilkan jumlah yang Anda tabung. Ini visualisasi progress yang mindful dan calming.
Apakah Moonbow gratis?
Ya, 100% gratis. Tidak ada biaya tersembunyi, tidak ada premium tier, tidak ada iklan. Moonbow adalah alat open-source untuk semua orang yang ingin financial peace.
Bisakah saya akses Moonbow offline?
Ya! Setelah halaman pertama kali dimuat, Moonbow bisa digunakan sepenuhnya offline. Semua fitur tetap berfungsi tanpa koneksi internet.
Bagaimana cara reset semua data?
Buka Developer Tools browser (F12), masuk ke Console, ketik: localStorage.clear() lalu refresh halaman. Semua data akan terhapus dan Moonbow kembali seperti baru.
Apakah ada backup data?
Karena data tersimpan di localStorage, tidak ada auto-backup. Kami sarankan screenshot berkala atau catat data penting secara manual. Fitur export/import sedang dalam pengembangan.
Filosofi "Not control. Connection." itu artinya apa?
Budgeting tradisional fokus pada kontrol dan pembatasan. Moonbow percaya pendekatan yang lebih sehat adalah membangun kesadaran dan koneksi dengan kebiasaan finansial Anda. Ketika Anda paham pola pengeluaran tanpa judgment, perubahan terjadi secara natural.

Welcome to Moonbow

Finding your arc of light in the darkness

"A Moonbow is a rare rainbow that appears at night, when moonlight meets mist in perfect harmony. Unlike the bold daylight rainbow, it reveals itself softly—a gentle arc of color visible only to those patient enough to look. Your finances, too, need this gentle moonlit approach, not the harsh glare of control."
— The Moonbow Principle

🌈 The Moonbow Phenomenon

A Moonbow appears only when conditions align perfectly: a nearly full moon, mist or waterfall spray, and clear dark skies. It cannot be forced—only witnessed. Like this rare phenomenon, financial clarity emerges not from aggressive control, but from creating the right conditions: awareness, patience, and gentle observation. The Moonbow teaches us that the most beautiful transformations happen in softness, not severity.

🌙 Wisdom of the Moonbow

🌕 The Moonbow's Rarity
"A Moonbow appears only when three elements align perfectly: a full moon's light, water droplets in the air, and a clear night sky opposite the moon. Force any element, and the phenomenon vanishes. Your financial harmony is the same—it emerges when conditions align: clear intention (moon), disciplined action (mist), and peaceful patience (night sky). You cannot rush a Moonbow. You can only create the space for it to reveal itself."
✨ The Moonbow's Gentleness
"Unlike a daytime rainbow's vivid colors, a Moonbow appears pale, subtle, almost ghostly. The human eye can barely detect its hues—they exist more as a whisper than a shout. Traditional budgeting screams with numbers, rules, and judgments. Moonbow budgeting whispers through awareness. The loudest financial transformations often come from the gentlest observations. What appears faint to the untrained eye becomes luminous to those who practice seeing softly."
🌑 The Moonbow's Darkness Paradox
"Here lies the profound truth: a Moonbow exists only in darkness. It cannot appear in daylight—the very brightness that reveals most things conceals this phenomenon. Your financial struggles, your 'dark' periods of debt or scarcity, are not obstacles to clarity. They are the necessary darkness in which your Moonbow can finally be seen. The path to financial peace does not avoid the darkness—it learns to find the arc of light within it."
👁️ The Moonbow's Invisibility
"Of all who stand beneath a Moonbow sky on any given night, most will never see it—not because it isn't there, but because they were not looking, or did not know such a thing could exist. The Moonbow does not announce itself. It does not demand to be seen. It simply is. Your financial progress is the same: the impulse purchase quietly declined, the small saving added without fanfare, the anxious spending pattern gently noticed and released. These do not appear on any scoreboard. No one applauds. The world will not reflect them back to you. But they are real, and they are accumulating—invisible to all except the one who knows to look. Do not measure your journey by what others can see. Measure it by what you are learning to see in yourself."

🌟 Getting Started in 3 Steps

1 Start with Your "Why"
Go to Moodboard → Upload an image that represents financial peace for you. Set a milestone. This is your North Star.
2 Track with Awareness
Use Cash Flow daily to note income & expenses. Add your mood. Don't judge—just observe. Patterns will emerge.
3 Reflect & Adjust
Weekly: Review your Cash Flow notes. Monthly: Check Saving Tracker progress. Yearly: Complete your Financial Reflection. The practice is the progress.

💡 Pro Tips

  • Your data is saved automatically in your browser. No sign-up needed.
  • Dark mode available (moon icon, top right) for night budgeting.
  • All features work offline. Track anytime, anywhere.
  • There's no "right way" to use Moonbow. Trust your intuition.
  • Progress isn't linear. Some months will be hard. That's okay.
